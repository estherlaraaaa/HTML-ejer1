With the best of the season coming from "orchards, farms and gardens," NPR has put together an impressive collection of 10 summer cookbooks

Summer is, after all, so often time of accidental vegetarianism. It's when even carnivorous folk (myself among them) are seduced by fields and farms stands, lured in by lettuce and temped by tomatoes, We don't necessarilt set out go meatless. But a couple of fruit smoothis and several bowls of baby greens later in the lazy heat of a stove-avoiding afternoon, that's just what happens. And so, to help you make the most of summer's bounty, here are 10 cookbooks that take their inspirations from that most peaceful of kingdoms -the plant kingdom.
